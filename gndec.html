About GNDEC

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956, is a premier engineering institution in Northern India. It is ISO certified, NBA accredited, and holds autonomous status from UGC, ensuring high academic standards. GNDEC is committed to technical education, research, and innovation, offering a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The college collaborates with leading industries and international universities, fostering global exposure and practical learning. With state-of-the-art laboratories, modern infrastructure, and experienced faculty, GNDEC empowers students with the skills needed to excel in engineering, entrepreneurship, and leadership, making a significant impact on society and industry.
